KWC Courses

KWC is committed to providing a low cost education programme to women on the Kapiti Coast. Each term KWC offers a range of courses. These cover a variety of topics including how to be the best parent we can; developing new life skills; finding out more about who we are and who we may want to be; developing better home management skills; keeping ourselves well and healthy; creating beautiful things. Alongside most courses KWC offers onsite childcare.
